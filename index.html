mission

To deliver the highest quality sales, marketing and regulatory organisation to maximise pharmaceutical and healthcare brands to their full potential. To be the partner of choice for multinational pharmaceutical and healthcare companies in the MENA Region.

Some words about Dayarn Pharma

Dayarn Pharma, founded in 2010 and headquartered in Dubai, is a multi-national style organisation with the aim to deliver the highest quality standards of sales, marketing and regulatory activities needed to maximise brands in the pharmaceutical and healthcare markets across the MENA Region.

Our success is based on employing and training the highest quality people from multinational companies. We're proud of our team and they are one of the reasons Dayarn Pharma is now a trusted and reliable partner to several multinationals in the region.

What we do

Dayarn Pharma delivers all aspects of the sales, marketing, regulatory and pricing functions needed to maximise pharmaceutical and healthcare brands to their full potential. The strategic and tactical plans are implemented to the highest standards, delivering success in some of the most challenging markets in the world.

our team

Dayarn Pharma's management team comprises leading former executives of some of the largest multi-national pharmaceutical companies in the world. Their experience spans all critical functions needed to excel in the region.

Omar Khan

Founder & CEO

Omar Khan has over 30 years of experience in pharmaceuticals in senior strategic and operational posts. Prior to Dayarn Pharma, he was Head of Marketing and Sales Middle East, Africa and Eastern Europe for Novartis. For the previous eighteen years, Omar was with Boehringer Ingelheim where he held a variety of roles. He was Head of Sales for Boehringer UK, before being promoted to Head of UK Strategic Operations. Following his UK success, Omar was posted to German headquarters, where he held a number of positions; As Regional Business Manager he was responsible for delivering the P&L for the Middle East, Africa, Pakistan and Taiwan territories.
